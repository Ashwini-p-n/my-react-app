Standard Breakpoints:

  • xl: 1280px and up
  • 2xl: 1536px and up

Max-width Breakpoints:

  • max-sm: below 640px
  • max-md: below 768px
  • max-lg: below 1024px
  • max-xl: below 1280px
  • max-2xl: below 1536px
